Align Content Exercise

In this exercise, you'll practice the align-content property. This property controls how multiple lines of flex items are distributed along the cross axis. The container below has eight items that wrap into multiple lines. Currently, align-content is set to stretch.

Tasks:

  1. Open style.css and change align-content to flex-start. Observe how lines align to the top of the container.
  2. Change it to flex-end. See lines align to the bottom.
  3. Change it to center. Notice lines centered in the container.
  4. Change it to space-between. See equal space between lines.
  5. Change it to space-around. Observe space around each line.
  6. Change it to space-evenly. See even spacing including edges.
  7. Change back to stretch to see lines stretch to fill the container.
  8. Experiment with different values and see the effects.
