Align Items Exercise

In this exercise, you'll practice the align-items property. This property controls how flex items are aligned along the cross axis. The container below has four items of different heights. Currently, align-items is set to stretch.

Tasks:

  1. Open style.css and change align-items to flex-start. Observe how items align to the top of the container.
  2. Change it to flex-end. See items align to the bottom.
  3. Change it to center. Notice items centered vertically.
  4. Change it to baseline. See how items align based on their text baseline.
  5. Change back to stretch to see items stretch to fill the container height.
  6. Experiment with different values and see the effects.
